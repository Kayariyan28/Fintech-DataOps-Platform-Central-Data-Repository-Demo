## Slide 2: The Market Context

### The Fintech "Speed Limit"
*   **Customer Expectations:** Instant approvals, real-time insights, personalized offers.
*   **Regulatory Pressure:** Stricter compliance (GDPR, Basel III) requires absolute data lineage.
*   **The Bottleneck:** Legacy data systems cannot keep up with the velocity of modern finance.

> **Key Insight:** The winners of the next decade will be defined by their ability to turn data into decisions in milliseconds, not days.

## Slide 4: Business Value Drivers

### 1. Risk Mitigation
*   **Real-time Fraud Detection:** Catching anomalies *during* the transaction, not after settlement.
*   **Impact:** Estimated **15% reduction** in fraud losses annually.

### 2. Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV)
*   **Dynamic Credit Scoring:** Incorporating alternative data (Academic, Utilities) to approve credit-invisible customers.
*   **Impact:** **20% increase** in addressable market.

### 3. Operational Agility
*   **Automated Quality Gates:** "Bad data" is stopped at the door.
*   **Impact:** Data Scientists spend **90%** of time on modeling, up from 20%.
